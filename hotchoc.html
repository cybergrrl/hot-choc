Hot Chocolate

How to make a divine creamy hot chocolate

Ingredients

  • 250 g dark chocolate (min. 70%)
  • 350 ml whole milk
  • 250 ml whipping cream
  • 3 table spoons icing sugar
  • optional: 1 table spoon instant espresso
  • cocoa powder for decoration

Preparation

  1. Finely chop the chocolate. The finer, the better, so it can melt well.
  2. Mix milk, 120 ml cream, powdered sugar and the optional espresso powder in a saucepan. Then heat over medium heat, but do not bring to a boil. If bubbles start forming on the edge and/or it starts smoking a little, pull the pot off the stove.
  3. Add chopped chocolate and stir with a wooden spoon until it has completely melted.
  4. Whip the remaining cream to desired consistency (we recommnd lightly whipped but fully whip if you prefer)
  5. Fill the hot chocolate in two mugs.
  6. Pour cream on top and dust with cocoa powder.
  7. Enjoy!
